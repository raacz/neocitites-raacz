- **System Default.** Devices with a preference for light theme will be met with Higher Contrast Light Mode. Devices with a preference for a dark theme will be met with Higher Contrast Dark Mode. 
- **Higher Contrast Light Mode and Dark Mode** use colour combinations that meet <a href="https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG22/Understanding/contrast-minimum.html">Web Content Accessibility Guidelines</a> minimum thresholds for colour contrast. 
- **Lower Contrast Light Mode and Dark Mode** use colour combinations that are occasionally lower than these minimum thresholds. Lower contrast modes are intended to be more suitable for reading at night-time. Some individuals might find them less overwhelming than the higher-contrast modes. Image brightness is also reduced in these modes.

Clicking anywhere else on the page will dismiss the theme selector pop up.
