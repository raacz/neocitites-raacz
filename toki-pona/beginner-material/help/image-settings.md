title: Image Settings
permalink: false
---

Show or hide the image, the image's description, and the image's attribution.
- <strong>Image Description.</strong> In addition to serving as an accessibility feature, image descriptions may draw attention to certain aspects of the image that are relevant to the story. In this way, they might provide an extra hint. 
- <strong>Attribution.</strong> All images used by Beginner Stories are either part of the Public Domain, or used under Creative Commons licenses. Each attribution includes a link to the original image, the name of the creator, and a link to the specific license used.
