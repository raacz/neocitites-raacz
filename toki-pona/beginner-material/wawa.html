These Quest Texts are written in casual, idiomatic Toki Pona and range from fictional to informational.

More information

While Bite-Sized and Topic texts are written to be as understandable as possible, these higher-level texts may meander and use a more conversational, flowy style. In this way, they are more similar to the kind of Toki Pona you will encounter in immersion settings.
