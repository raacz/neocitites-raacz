Reading List

About the Reading List

Do you enjoy gamification? Need motivation to read?

Your challenge, should you choose to accept it, is to read every single story on this website. Upon enabling the reading list, this page will be filled with large charts listing every single text, each with an 'X' next to them.

Additionally, when you visit any beginner text, a small checkbox will appear at the bottom of every page. You can check that box. Returning to this page, it update that text from an 'X' to a checkmark!

You will also be able to view exactly how many words you've read, how many texts you've read, and what percentage of the website you've completed!

Progress on your reading list will be saved to your device. You can choose to close the reading list at any time and wipe out all your reading data.
