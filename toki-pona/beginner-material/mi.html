Reading Tracker

Welcome

The Reading Tracker, as the name suggests, provides a way to track your reading.
When activated, you can mark stories as completed.

Stats measured include:

  • Total Words Read
  • Total Pages Read
  • Percentage of the Site Completed
  • Achievements Earned.

You can pause the tracker at any time without resetting your progress.
But if you want to reset your progress, you can also do that too.

Would you like to try it out?

About

How To Track

The stories that you mark as read will be saved directly to your browser. If you view Beginner Texts in Toki Pona in incognito mode, or using a different browser, or a different device, you will not be able to access your progress.

New stories being released to the site will not effect the stories that you've already read.

Philosophy Behind the Tracker

Please use this site however you like. The Reading Tracker encourages you to read every text on the site, but not all the stories may be appealing or even enjoyable for you.

I've included this because I'm the kind of person who, as a voracious reader in my youth, liked to keep lists of books that I've read as a matter of principle.

I know that if I was just starting out on my reading journey, I would have appreciated something like this. But you might be a different kind of person, and that's totally okay! Feel free to use the Reading Tracker only to the extent that is motivating and sustainable. Also, feel free to take a break from it by pressing the 'Exit the Challenge' button in the top right of this page.

Measuring Progress and Detailled Statistics

Progress is measured as the amount of words you've read compared to the total amount of words available on the site.

If you are techy and are itching to get even more detailed statistics, take a look at the output from the console. Also, there is likely a way for you to download and upload your own localStorage if you are super keen on not losing your progress.
