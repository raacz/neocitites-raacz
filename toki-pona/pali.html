Delivered in English:

  • mooo. (2022-ongoing), a series of raging vignettes about Toki Pona and myself

  • Intermediate Toki Pona: Building from jan Misali's Twelve Days (August 2021), a toki pona grammar crash course for those with some familiarity with the language already

lipu / writing:

lipu mi li lon lipu.pona.la kin.

  • jan Posepomamuka#52208 o, la kulupu nasa pi toki pona la jan nasa li wile e pali tan jan sin. pali ni li tawa utala pi lipu musi lili.
  • tu lukin lon la kulupu Kalasasi li lon ma kasi li kama lukin e akesi suli pi tawa noka. o sona e ni: toki musi ni li ike tan ni: jan ala li ken sona e kon ona. mi taso li sona.
  • kama pi poki ala la mi sitelen e toki musi lili lon tenpo sike#MAM
  • lipu musi pi wawa lili la toki musi lili luka wan li kepeken toki pona pi wawa lili li ken pona tawa jan sin (tenpo sike#MAMTT).
