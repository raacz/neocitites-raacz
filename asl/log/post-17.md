### First Video


[10 Ways To Sign "Live" in ASL (Vocabulary Expansion Series, Part 73), Dr. Bill with student: Cassie](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8ntQGtULaFo)


#### Notes
- Approach and face-to-face are different glossing for the same concept.
- There is like a 'here' but jutted out 
- Recording is an old sign for 'computer' derived from the computer-buffering symbol done downwards
- intern is a modestly done Industry
- common can be done like 'everyday'

### Second Video

[ ASL signs for victim, vodka, vanilla, vibrator, etc... (Vocabulary Expansion Series, Episode 80) ](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Byd_kHEVETg&t=17s)

#### Notes
- older sign for 'victim' is STUCK with no nmm. current paradigms for victim include a shaked v and a v at the side of neck. 
- shaked v can be a vibrator too..., or vanilla, or vitamin...
- ice cream can be done with an X-style E
- v at side of neck can be vodka, which also has like an 8 version and a 1 version (which is very similar looking to 'deaf accent'). vicars also notes the international version of vodka: c-swirled. 

#### Vocab
- Therapy
- Victim
- Vitamin
